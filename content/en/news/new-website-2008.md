We're glad to announce the **new Azzurra website**.

As you can see, we've completely refreshed the look of the site and the forum, and we've also introduced a new **ChanList** along with an updated visual style for the **ChanStats** service.

The CMS in use was developed in-house by the Azzurra staff.

## ChanList

The **ChanList** service is up and running. Founders of any channels registered on Azzurra not yet in the listing can ask for inclusion by contacting the staff in `#IRCHelp`.
